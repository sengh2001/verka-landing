Who We Are

Punjab's Most Trusted
Dairy Brand — Now On Campus

Verka is the flagship brand of MILKFED Punjab — a cooperative renowned for delivering fresh, pure, and affordable dairy products across northern India for over 50 years.

Our franchise inside IIT Ropar Campus brings this legacy of quality directly to students, faculty, and staff. Every product is sourced fresh daily from MILKFED's certified processing plants, ensuring you get the purest dairy with zero compromises.

Whether it's a glass of cold lassi after a lab session or a tub of paneer for hostel cooking — we've got you covered.
